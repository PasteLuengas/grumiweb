About Me

Hi there! I'm Thomas Parra, a music producer and composer with four years of industry experience. certified in music theory, composition, and mixing and mastering on digital workstations, I have honed my skills over the years to create dynamic and engaging music. Additionally, I have dedicated three years to perfecting my compositional techniques to create unique and memorable music. With five years of guitar and two years of piano learning, I have a well-rounded musical education that informs my creative process. My portfolio showcases examples of my work in film music and sound effects, as well as my original compositions created through years of self-learning and dedicated study.

Education

I am proud to hold a certificate in music theory from AulaFacil.com, having completed a 40-hour course. Throughout the course, I was able to deepen my knowledge of music theory and enhance my understanding of key concepts in the field. I worked hard to achieve a final grade of 7.5, which is a testament to my dedication and commitment to my music education. This certificate represents my passion for music and my desire to continue learning and growing as a musician and composer. See certificate.
I am proud to have obtained a certificate in music and film effects provided and issued by UNICEF through the AGORA program. This course allowed me to explore the world of music in film and learn how musical effects can influence emotions and storytelling. The certificate is a testament to my dedication to learning and expanding my knowledge in the field of music. It represents my passion for music and my commitment to improving as a musician and composer. See certificate.
I have a solid musical background of five years of learning guitar and two years of piano. I also have extensive experience in music production, having spent four years learning about composing, mixing and mastering on digital workstations, and three years perfecting compositional techniques. In addition, I have spent four years self-taught through non-certified courses and different media.
